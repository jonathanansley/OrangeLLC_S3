Meet our Team

Patrick Modlin

Founder




















Patrick Modlin is the co-founder of Orange LLC, an aviation technical services company providing aviation safety, aircrew services, and range support. He is a graduate of the United States Naval Academy, the Naval Test Pilot School, a retired flight tester, and a proud combat veteran. He has unmanned experience in directing the Navy’s Threat/Targets Systems Department, conducting hundreds of worldwide target operations with an extensive force of unmanned vehicles (air, land, and sea). He has been accountable for budgets over $1B and led hundreds of service members and government civilians in Test and Evaluation roles ranging from the Program Executive Office to Test Resource Managers. His most recent experience is with AVIAN's aviation safety work at (Google) X’s Loon LLC in Mountain View, CA.

Matt Ansley

Founder




















Matt “Torch” Ansley is the co-founder of Orange LLC, an aviation test support service company providing safety, aircrew, and range support services. He is a retired Naval Aviation tester specializing in P-3, C-130, and UAS aircraft. Over the last 10 years he typically supported a dozen different test programs per year at VX-30, Pt Mugu. As a seasoned aviation safety officer, he stood up the aviation safety program for NAVAIR’s Threat/Target Systems Department. He recently transitioned from the VX-30 Contract Aircrew site lead to the position of Aviation Safety Coordinator for AVIAN’s work with Google X’s Project Loon in Mountain View, CA.
